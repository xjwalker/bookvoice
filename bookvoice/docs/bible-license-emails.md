## 1. ESV — Crossway

**How to apply:**
- Fill out the **digital permissions form**: https://www.crossway.org/permissions/digital/
- For follow-up or questions: **licensing@crossway.org**

**Important note:** Crossway says they "license to organizations, not to individuals or solo developers." Consider framing this as a community/ministry project serving blind users, not a solo dev side project.

**What to say in the form:**

Hi, my name is Jesus Kevin Walker Ortiz. I'm building a free mobile app called ReadItForMe. The app uses text-to-speech to read books aloud — I started building it because I have friends who are blind and wanted to give them an easy way to listen to the Bible on their phone without needing internet.

The app works fully offline. The Bible text is stored on the device and read aloud through the phone's built-in speech engine. The text isn't displayed on screen for reading or exported anywhere — it's purely an audio experience. The whole UI is built with VoiceOver and TalkBack support so blind users can navigate it independently.

I'd like to include the ESV as one of the available translations. Right now the app ships with the KJV and Reina-Valera 1909 (both public domain). I want to offer the ESV because many of the people I'm building this for prefer it as their daily Bible.

The app is and will remain completely free — no ads, no subscriptions, no monetization of any kind. I'm happy to include whatever copyright notice or attribution you require, and I can send you a build of the app if you'd like to see how it works.

## 3. RV1960 & TLA — Sociedades Bíblicas

**How to apply:**
- The RV1960 and TLA rights are held by national Bible Societies (Sociedades Bíblicas Unidas network). Contact your **local/regional Bible Society** directly.
- Search for: "Sociedad Bíblica de [your country]" — e.g., Sociedad Bíblica de Colombia, Sociedad Bíblica de México, etc.
- Their umbrella websites (sociedadesbiblicas.org, unitedbiblesocieties.org) were unreachable when last checked. Try reaching out via their social media pages or local offices.

**What to say (Spanish):**
